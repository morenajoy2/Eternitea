About us

Eternitea is a food service firm that began operations in 2010 and specializes in healthy milk tea. The notion is primarily inspired by Asia's distinct tradition of tea drinking, which is a vital component of a ceremonial in entertaining clients and represents a symbol of respect and value for the guests in many Asian nations. Eternitea tea beverages are guaranteed to be created from 100 percent freshly brewed high-quality loose-leaf teas, made interesting by various flavors and combinations for a fun and healthy living.
